Project Idea

Why We Chose Mali

We selected this idea because budget tracking is useful for students and everyday users. The website aims to make expense recording simple, clear, and accessible.

Project Goals

-Simplify expense tracking for students and everyday users

-Provide a clean and user-friendly experience

-Help users understand and manage their spending habits

-Encourage smarter budgeting and saving
